About

A hard conversation that started with I don't know how to say this and ended with I'm proud of you.

Saturday morning under the car, learning which bolts to tighten, and getting a free four letter vocabulary lesson.

Chin up, shoulders back, look them in the eyes and shake hands firmly.

For a lot of people in our community, these moments never happened. The knowledge didn't get passed on. The conversation never came.

Dad, Can You? exists to fill that gap.
